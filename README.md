Asset Curator is a practical asset management app designed for organisations and users who need a reliable way to track physical equipment, monitor location changes, manage maintenance, and generate useful reports.

With Asset Curator you can:

- **Track assets with unique IDs and QR codes.** Each item can be labelled, scanned, and updated quickly.
- **Record key asset details.** Store title, description, serial number, part number, locations, tags, and status.
- **Monitor where assets belong and where they currently are.** This makes it easier to identify out-of-place or missing items.
- **Use maintenance profiles and routine servicing.** Track due dates, hours, cycles, and distance-based servicing.
- **Generate reporting insights.** Review asset summaries, status distribution, location coverage, and maintenance activity.
- **Work locally or across multiple devices.** Paid tiers can use shared master database syncing for multi-user workflows.
